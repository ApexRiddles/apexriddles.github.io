I N S T R U C T I O N S

Happy to see that you're still here with us. Let's go over some rules now, shall we?

1. Apex Riddles consists of multiple levels, with varying levels of difficulty. Don't get diappointed if you can't solve the problems - with time and skill, each level can be understood and solved.

2. Don't attempt to spoil answers for yourself or anyone else, for that matter. Hints are often provided in each level, and you can always use your favourite search engine for support. If you are really stuck, we do offer you three lifelines, so you have the chance to email us and recieve another hint.

3. The answers consist of a URL that you need to replace before the .html.

4. Most of all, we want you to have fun. All levels start easy, but gradually increase in difficulty. Guessing the answers at random likely won't help, so make sure you have your friends and resouces for support.

If you're ready to start, then go ahead and click below. Sometimes, it's the distractions that can get in the way.
